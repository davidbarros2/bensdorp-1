This is the heaviest module in Phase 3: it covers DATA-03 (auto_adjust=True), DATA-04 (220-day history), DATA-08 (ticker normalization period↔hyphen), DATA-09 (3-retry exponential backoff), and DATA-10 (95% coverage check).
